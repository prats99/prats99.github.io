About

Official Logo

MANCHESTER UNITED FC logo is one of the most recognizable crests in the world, being recognised even by people that don't follow football…The badge is derived from the crest of city of MANCHESTER while the devil on the club badge stems for the club's nickname ‘THE RED DEVILS’, which was adopted in the early 1960's after the great MATT BUSBY heard it in reference to the red shirted Salford rugby league side. The ship has something to do with the introduction of the Manchester Shipping Canal. Manchester was a big industrial city but ships had to dock in Liverpool because it was a massive port, with the introduction of the canal in Manchester it meant anything going to Manchester could go directly through the canal instead of going to Liverpool, something that has given rise to the massive rivalry with Liverpool!
